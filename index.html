Raw Materials

What role do raw materials have?

Raw materials play an important role in the production process of the company! We have sourced our raw materials from farmers of Belagavi, Karnataka. This avoids cheating from middle-men and is also quality ensured!



Self Care Products

The raw materials required, in no specific order are:

½ cup almond oil

¼ cup coconut oil

¼ cup beeswax

1 tsp vitamin E oil

2 TBSP shea butter (or cocoa butter, optional)

essential oils

vanilla extract / rose water



Procedure

- Over a double boiler, melt and stir almond, coconut oil, vitamin e oil, beeswax

- Once melted, add scents of vanilla extract, rose water, shea butter and essential oil

- Store in a glass jar. For best results, use within 6 months

Reusable, Recyclable Cups!

Newspaper

Mixing Bowl

Water

Flour

Acrylic Paint

Scissors

Paintbush





Procedure

- Make paper maché by mixing flour and water paste to newspaper strips

- Stick to plastic bottle for a mould. Remove when dry

- Line the inside, lid and straw with resin

- Once dried and designed, this will last up to 10 months!


Eco-Friendly and Healthy

Our team has replaced plastic with resin. Resin is made of organic compounds and does not react with liquids, the way plastic does! It is much more eco-friendly and it has advanced health benefits.

We are providing straws also. These are paper straws coated with non-toxic resin and thus are very eco-friendly! (Rs. 32 for our straws)

Investment

What is the investment we require?

Our team asks for an investment of Rs. 500, per investor. The total investment is Rs. 3,000

Where are we going to use this investment?

We are using Rs. 1543 (77.15 * 20) for the cosmetics, Rs. 980 (49 * 20) for the paper maché, Rs. 200 for advertisement, Rs. 200 for individual packaging of products. This leave Rs. 77 as our contingency fund

Where are we using our profits?

We are giving 100% of our profits to an organisation, Sandesh that works with the special children and adults. It is located in Lingarajpuram, Bangalore.

How are these profits benefit society?

The sales proceeds will be used to buy good quality raw materials and contribute towards benefit of society

How can you ensure the quality considering the price?

Our research and development team constantly monitors the quality of materials purchased for production. We also test new raw materials for better quality at lower prices.
